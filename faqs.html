Frequently Ask Questions

1. What is the purpose of the Remote Controlled Fish Farm Cleaner?
Ans: The device is designed to clean fish tanks or ponds by removing waste, algae, and debris using a remotely operated system, helping to maintain a healthier environment for fish.
2. How is the cleaner controlled?
Ans: cleaner is operated remotely using a controller (cellphone), allowing the user to move the device and activate cleaning functions without direct contact with the water.
3. Is the device safe for fish?
Ans: the design ensures that all cleaning components move gently and avoid direct contact with the fish to prevent harm while cleaning.
4. What are the benefits of using this prototype?
Ans: It reduces manual labor, improves water quality, prevents fish diseases caused by dirty environments, and increases efficiency in managing fish farms.
