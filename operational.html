Operational Guides

I. Electrical Wiring Connection
Step 1: In the top section of the device, lift the top cover of the control box.
Step 2: Carefully place the batteries on the battery holders.
Step 3: Attach the two propellers to each designated connector.
Step 4: Turn on the three switches located inside and outside of the control box.
Step 5: Close the control box.
II. Operating the Device
Step 1: Turn on Bluetooth in the phone settings.
Step 2: Check the “ available devices” on the Bluetooth. Choose the device name “HC-05”.
Step 3: Enter the passcode (1234) and pair.
Step 4: Open the app and go to the “ Bluetooth devices”.
Step 5: Find the name of the device “ HC-05” and Connect.
Step 6: Check the indicator or listen to the sound. If the light turns on, it means it's connected. If not, go back to step 1.
